FAQ Section

All you need to know before applying to our Full Stack Program.

01 Are there any scholarships available?

+

Yes, we offer several scholarships for eligible students based on merit and need.

02 Are there any Trial Classes?

+

Yes, we offer trial classes so you can experience our teaching methods before enrolling.

03 Is it right for a non-technical person?

+

Our program is designed to be accessible to individuals from all backgrounds, including non-technical ones.

04 I'm over 40 years old, should I apply?

+

Absolutely! We welcome applicants of all ages and believe learning is for everyone.

05 Eligibility

+

Eligibility criteria vary by program; please check the specific requirements on our website.

06 Do you offer career support?

+

Yes, we provide extensive career support including resume reviews, mock interviews, and job placement assistance.

07 Is it possible to share our job offer(s) with your Vvork?

+

Yes, employers are welcome to share job offers with us to connect with our talented graduates.

08 Do you organize recruitment events?

+

Yes, we regularly organize recruitment events where employers can meet our students.
